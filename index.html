Professional Summary

I work as a Systems Analyst, supporting enterprise systems while building on my experience as an NSOC Analyst, monitoring 200+ business networks in real time, triaging incidents, and escalating major incidents to keep services operational and downtime to a minimum. Daily, I utilize SolarWinds, Cisco vManage, Juniper Mist, and Citrix to track availability and performance issues, then validate their impact and work with the appropriate teams or vendors to resolve the issues. On the security front, I work as an L2 analyst, utilizing Microsoft Defender and Microsoft Sentinel to resolve escalated events that third-party providers are unable to resolve, confirm what is legitimate versus noise, and drive the next steps through containment and escalation as needed.

Career Focus & Opportunities

My main area of interest is governance, risk, and compliance, and I am searching for opportunities that allow me to link day-to-day security work to risk choices, control implementation, and audit preparation. At the same time, I am interested in hands-on roles in blue team operations, SOC or NSOC, cloud security, and security engineering. Through my internship and personal projects, I wrote policies, drew diagrams, and created security documentation, all of which helped to build my GRC foundation. I am now studying for the ISC2 Certified in Governance, Risk, and Compliance (CGRC) exam and pursuing the Cyber AB CMMC assessor path.
